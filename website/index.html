Welcome to Commander Genius

Commander Genius, also known as CloneKeen Plus, is an engine which interprets the Commander Keen Invasion of the Vorticons series. As fans and developers, we try to implement new features, improve the gameplay, and give players an experience that feels like playing the original game. You will also need the original game files in addition to Commander Genius. Commander Keen: Marooned on Mars is shareware and may be freely, and legally downloaded elsewhere. Commander Keen: The Earth Explodes and Commander Keen: Keen Must Die! are registered versions which can be purchased from 3D Realms, Apogee, or purchased from someone willing to sell you their copy. If you still have these old games, then give Commander Genius a try! You will be surprised at how good they look and how much fun they are to play!

Goodbye Galaxy and Keen Dreams support

We are working hard on the Commander Genius support for the Goodbye Galaxy series, Keen Dreams, and also mods like Keen 7-9.

Every update brings new features and more support. We try to do a version update once per month, so you can be sure you always have the newest, coolest features.

Not just Windows, Linux, and Mac but other platforms are supported as well, and more will be supported in the future!

Invasion of the Vorticons support

This series is mostly bug free, and the physics are very accurate. If you want to experience Commander Keen Invasion of the Vorticons with some improvements yet classic gameplay, Commander Genius is the program you want!

Features

  • 4 Player Mode
  • Fully customizable button layout
  • Graphic Scalers
  • OpenGL Support
  • Improved CloneKeen engine. Only small bits of it are left
  • 3 difficulty modes in all the Commander Keen games
  • Mod support
  • Automatic extraction of data (sounds, story, etc.) from the exe-files
  • In-game Music support (Yes, music in the Invasion of the Vorticons series! And don't just trust us, it's sweet!)
  • New graphical effects
  • Much faster performance, because there is no emulation
  • HUD in Invasion of the Vorticons
  • High Quality Pack graphics and sound support
  • and much more...
